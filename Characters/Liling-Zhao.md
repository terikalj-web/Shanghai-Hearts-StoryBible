- Curious

- Affectionate

- Adaptable

---

## Weaknesses

- Impulsive
- Easily distracted
- Avoids conflict

## Core Motivation

Happiness.

Liling wants the people she loves to be happy.

Including herself.

Unlike many members of her social circle, she refuses to believe misery is a requirement for success.

---

## Biggest Fear

Watching her family drift apart.

Losing the closeness they share.

---

## Relationship With Lihuan

Liling absolutely adores her.

Growing up, Lihuan was the cool sister.

The adventurous sister.

The one who disappeared to America and came back with stories.

### Private Opinion

Lihuan deserves to choose her own future.

Anyone who disagrees can argue with Liling.

---

## What People Get Wrong About Liling

People assume Liling is shallow.

She isn't.

People assume she only cares about fashion, parties, and gossip.

She doesn't.

People assume she misses important things.

In reality, she notices almost everything.

She simply packages her observations as jokes.

---

## Character Arc

### Book 1

Acts as a source of humor, warmth, and emotional support.

Often provides observations that are more accurate than people realize.

### Book 2 Potential

May begin stepping out of the role of "youngest sister" and establishing her own identity.

Could become unexpectedly influential within the family.

---

## Story Function

The Sunshine.

Represents joy, warmth, family, and optimism.

Often lightens heavier scenes while still delivering surprisingly sharp observations.

Frequently says the thing everyone else is afraid to say.
