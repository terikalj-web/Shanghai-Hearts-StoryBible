# Jun Zhao

## Basic Information

| Field | Details |
|---|---|
| Full Name | Zhao Jun |
| Chinese Name | 赵骏 |
| Age | 18 |
| Nationality | Chinese |
| Occupation | University Student |
| Family Position | Youngest Zhao Sibling / Only Brother |

---

## Physical Description

- Tall
- Athletic build
- Broad shoulders
- Naturally handsome
- Strong resemblance to his father
- Usually dressed more casually than the rest of the family
- Still carries some youthful energy despite attempting to appear older

---

## Personality

### Core Traits

- Loyal
- Protective
- Competitive
- Honest
- Impulsive
- Energetic
- Family-oriented

### Public Persona

The Zhao son.

Confident.

Popular.

Slightly reckless.

Often underestimated because of his age.

### Private Reality

Feels pressure to prove himself.

Constantly trying to be viewed as an adult.

Wants to protect his sisters but is often the one being protected.

---

## Strengths

- Loyal
- Courageous
- Honest
- Protective
- Quick learner
- Good judge of character

---

## Weaknesses

- Impulsive
- Stubborn
- Emotional
- Sometimes acts before thinking
- Hates feeling powerless

---

## Core Motivation

Belonging.

Jun wants to earn his place within the family rather than simply inherit it.

He wants to be respected, not protected.

---

## Biggest Fear

Being treated like a child.

Feeling unnecessary.

Watching something happen to his family and being unable to stop it.

---

## Relationship With Lihuan

Jun absolutely adores Lihuan.

Growing up, she was the sibling who always made time for him.

The one who listened.

The one who never treated him like an inconvenience.

### Private Opinion

Lihuan deserves whatever makes her happy.

Anyone who hurts her becomes his problem.

---

## Relationship With Meiying

Protective older sister.

Constantly worries about him.

Jun pretends to be annoyed by it.

Secretly likes it.

---

## Relationship With Liling

Partner in crime.

Most playful sibling relationship.

Usually responsible for family chaos.

---

## Relationship With Liang Zhan

Respects him.

Looks up to him.

Still occasionally tests him.

### Private Opinion

If Zhan hurts Lihuan, Jun will become a problem.

---

## Relationship With Ren

Thinks Ren is cool.

Probably more than he should.

Ren treats him like an equal, which immediately earns points.

### Private Opinion

Ren is either a terrible influence or an excellent one.

The answer changes daily.

---

## Habits

- Speaks before thinking
- Checks on his sisters constantly
- Volunteers for things he isn't qualified to do
- Acts tougher than he feels
- Pretends not to care when he absolutely does

---

## Speech Patterns

### Frequently Uses

- "Seriously?"
- "That's stupid."
- "I'm fine."
- "Relax."

### Communication Style

Direct.

Honest.

Occasionally sarcastic.

Rarely hides what he is thinking.

## What People Get Wrong About Jun

People assume he is immature.

He isn't.

People assume he doesn't understand what's happening around him.

He does.

People assume he is still a child.

That assumption usually irritates him.

---

## Character Arc

### Book 1

Acts as one of Lihuan's strongest supporters.

Begins stepping out from the shadow of his older siblings.

### Book 2 Potential

Learns what kind of man he wants to become.

May eventually become one of the most fiercely protective members of the Zhao family.

---

## Story Function

The Heart.

Represents family loyalty, youth, and unconditional love.

Often provides humor and warmth, but can become surprisingly serious when the people he loves are threatened.
