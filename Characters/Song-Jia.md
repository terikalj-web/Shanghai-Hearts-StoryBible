## Physical Description

- Polished appearance
- Sharp features
- Professional wardrobe
- Usually seen with a tablet, notebook, or phone
- Rarely appears rushed
- Maintains impeccable composure

---

## Personality

### Core Traits

- Intelligent
- Efficient
- Loyal
- Observant
- Dry sense of humor
- Practical
- Blunt when necessary

### Public Persona

The perfect assistant.

Professional.

Organized.

Unflappable.

The woman who somehow keeps Liang Zhan's life functioning.

### Private Reality

Far more opinionated than she appears.

Knows everyone's business.

Pretends not to.

Secretly enjoys watching wealthy people create their own problems.

---

## Strengths

- Exceptional organizational skills
- Reads people quickly
- Crisis management
- Attention to detail
- Loyal
- Discreet

---

## Weaknesses

- Workaholic
- Can be overly critical
- Struggles to delegate
- Occasionally forgets not everyone operates at her pace

---

## Core Motivation

Competence.

Song Jia takes pride in being the person others can rely on.

She values capability above status.

---

## Biggest Fear

Losing control of a situation she should have anticipated.

---

## Relationship With Liang Zhan

### Professional Relationship

Works as his executive assistant.

Responsible for managing much of his schedule, travel, and daily operations.

### Personal Relationship

One of the few people willing to challenge him.

Knows his habits.

Knows his tells.

Usually knows when he is upset before he does.

### Private Opinion

Zhan is brilliant.

Zhan is exhausting.

Both things can be true.

---

## Relationship With Lihuan

### First Impression

Problem.

Unexpected.

Concerning.

### What Changes

The more Song Jia gets to know Lihuan, the more she realizes she is not the problem.

The situation is.

Eventually becomes one of Lihuan's strongest supporters.

### Private Opinion

Lihuan makes Zhan happy.

Which is deeply inconvenient.

---

## Relationship With Ren

Friendly.

Suspicious.

Frequently annoyed.

Ren enjoys bothering her.

Song Jia pretends she dislikes him.

Nobody believes her.

---

## Relationship With Xu Nian

Mutual respect.

Professional admiration.

Recognizes how difficult Xu Nian's position truly is.

## Character Arc

### Book 1

Initially attempts to manage the consequences of Zhan's growing attachment to Lihuan.

Eventually becomes one of the few people quietly rooting for them.

### Book 2 Potential

Could become a trusted confidant to both Lihuan and Zhan.

May eventually confront her own tendency to prioritize everyone else's life over her own.

---

## Story Function

The Advisor.

Represents competence, perspective, and practicality.

Acts as both comic relief and a grounding force within the story.

Often sees problems before the main characters do.
